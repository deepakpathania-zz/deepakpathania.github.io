About me

My name is Deepak Pathania and I am a CSE undergrad at MSIT, GGSIPU, Delhi. I expect to graduate in the fall of 2018. I am primarily a front end web developer and UI/UX designer but I have a lot of interest in Python and Java development as well. I strive hard to learn something new every day.

A writer by heart, designer by will and geek by choice, I have worked as a freelancer content writer and graphic designer on several stand alone projects and was the university topper in the year 2016. I am an open source enthusiast and love contributing no matter how small or big the contribution is.

I love the hackathon culture because of the mentorship and adrenaline rush that comes along with it. I have been to about 6 hackathons this season and secured a position in 4 of them. I look forward to attending more of these in the near future. I have done quite a few internships and am looking for Summer'16 opportunities.
